The Origin Story

HOW IT
STARTED

It started with a simple frustration. I was broke — like actually broke — and I wanted to listen to music without ads interrupting every three songs. The free tier on Spotify was driving me insane. Apple Music wanted $11 a month. I didn't have $11 a month.

So I started thinking: why does music cost money to listen to at all? Artists upload their songs hoping people hear them. Listeners want to hear songs. Why is there a corporation sitting in the middle charging both sides?

I couldn't stop thinking about it. And then I realized — I could just build the thing I wanted to exist. I'm 13, I don't have money, I don't have a team, I don't have a degree. But I have time, I have an idea, and I have access to tools that make building easier than ever before.

FreeWave isn't just a music platform. It's proof that you don't need to wait until you're older, richer, or more qualified to start building something real. If you see a problem — fix it. That's the whole story.
