Got Questions?

FAQ

Is it really free for listeners? No catch?

Completely free, no catch. Listeners never pay anything. Our revenue comes entirely from artist promotion plans — not from listeners. You'll never see a paywall or ad.

How does FreeWave make money if it's free?

Artists pay for promotion features — getting featured in discovery, playlist placements, homepage rotations. Think of it like advertising, except it actually helps artists grow instead of annoying listeners.

Can I cancel my artist plan anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime with no penalties. Your music stays on the platform even on the free tier. You'll just lose access to paid promotion features when you downgrade.

Do I keep my music rights?

100%. FreeWave never takes ownership or licensing rights to your music. Upload it, remove it, license it elsewhere — it's always yours completely.

How does artist revenue sharing work?

A portion of FreeWave's overall platform revenue is distributed to artists monthly based on stream counts and engagement. Higher tier artists get a larger percentage of the pool.

Is there a discount for annual plans?

Yes — pay annually and save 20% across all artist plans. Toggle the billing switch above to see annual pricing. The savings add up fast on higher tiers.
